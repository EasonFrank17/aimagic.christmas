Stickman GTA City

Game Features

  • Open-world stickman action
  • Various vehicles to drive
  • Multiple missions and challenges
  • Customizable stickman character

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move
  • Press space to jump
  • Approach vehicles to enter
  • Complete missions for rewards

About Stickman GTA City

Stickman GTA City is an action-packed open-world game where you control a stickman character in a bustling city. Steal cars, complete missions, and explore the city while avoiding the police. With simple controls and addictive gameplay, this game offers hours of stickman mayhem.
