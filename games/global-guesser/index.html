Global Guesser

Game Features

  • Guess locations from around the world
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Interactive map with zoom functionality
  • Leaderboard to track your progress

How to Play

  1. Look at the image or description provided
  2. Guess the location on the map
  3. Submit your answer and see how close you were
  4. Earn points based on accuracy

About the Game

Global Guesser is an educational yet entertaining game that tests your knowledge of world geography. Challenge yourself to identify locations from around the globe and improve your geographical knowledge while having fun!
