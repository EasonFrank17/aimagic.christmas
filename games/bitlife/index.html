About BitLife

BitLife is an immersive life simulation game that puts you in control of your virtual character's destiny. From birth to death, every decision you make shapes your life story. Experience the ups and downs of life as you navigate through education, careers, relationships, and unexpected events. With its engaging gameplay and endless possibilities, BitLife offers a unique and entertaining way to explore different life paths and create your own legacy.
