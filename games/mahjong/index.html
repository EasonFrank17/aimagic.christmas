About Mahjong

Mahjong is a classic tile-matching game that challenges your memory and pattern recognition skills. Match identical tiles to remove them from the board and clear all tiles to win. With beautiful designs and relaxing gameplay, this game is perfect for unwinding while exercising your brain.
